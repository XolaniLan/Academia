Heritage Day
  • 09:00 AM - 09:30 PM
  • Speaker : Alexa Zone

Happy Heritage Day, South Africa! On this day, we celebrate the rich and diverse cultural heritage of our country. Let us use this day to celebrate our differences and to come together as a nation. Here are some ways to celebrate Heritage Day: Wear traditional clothing or jewelry. Visit a museum or cultural center. Attend a traditional dance performance or music concert. Cook and share traditional foods. Talk to your family and elders about your family history and traditions. Most importantly, enjoy this day with your loved ones!

Mentorship Programme
  • 10:00 AM - 09:00 PM
  • Speaker : Venil Monts

The Mzamomtsha Mentorship Program is a one-on-one relationship between a more experienced employee (mentor) and a less experienced employee (mentee). The goal of the program is to accelerate the mentee's professional development and help them achieve their career goals. Mentors provide their mentees with guidance, support, and advice. They can help mentees learn new skills, develop their career path, and navigate the company culture. Mentors can also help mentees build their professional network and prepare for leadership roles.

Technology Day
  • 11:00 AM - 09:00 PM
  • Speaker : Mavil Aven

Technology Day Statement Technology Day is a celebration of the incredible advancements in technology that have improved our lives. We thank the scientists, engineers, and entrepreneurs who work tirelessly to develop new technologies and solve some of the world's most pressing problems. This year, Technology Day is particularly significant as we continue to grapple with the challenges of the COVID-19 pandemic. Technology has played a vital role in helping us stay connected, work remotely, and access essential services during this difficult time. As we look to the future, let us commit to using technology to build a more inclusive, sustainable, and equitable world. Happy Technology Day!
